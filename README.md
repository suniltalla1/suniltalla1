### 🧑‍💻 About Me

Data Engineer with 4+ years of experience designing and building end-to-end data pipelines on Azure and AWS. I turn raw, messy data into clean, reliable, queryable datasets that power business decisions.

- 🔧 Building production data pipelines with **Python, PySpark, and SQL**
- ☁️ Cloud-native architectures on **Azure (ADF, Databricks, Synapse)** and **AWS**
- 🏗️ Automating infrastructure with **Terraform** and **CI/CD pipelines**
- 📊 Currently exploring **Microsoft Fabric** and **advanced Spark optimization**
- 📍 Based in **Texas, USA**
